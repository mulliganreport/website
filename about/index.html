About The Mulligan Report

Three guys who love golf decided the game deserved a better daily read.

The Short Version

The Mulligan Report is a free daily golf newsletter. Every morning before your tee time, we deliver the best stories from across the golf world: pro tour news, swing tips, gear reviews, original cartoons, hot takes, and the occasional rant about pace of play.

We're not a wire service. We're not regurgitating press releases. We read everything, pick the best, add our own perspective, and package it into something you actually want to read with your morning coffee.

Why We Started This

Golf media is broken. The big outlets are drowning in sponsored content and SEO filler. The forums are chaos. The YouTube algorithm shows you the same three swing tips on repeat. And nobody is putting together a clean, daily briefing that respects your time and actually sounds like it was written by someone who plays the game.

We wanted to build the newsletter we wish existed. Something that feels like your buddy texting you the best golf stuff he found that day, but with better writing and a daily cartoon.
