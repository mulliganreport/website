Privacy Policy

How we handle your data. The short version: we respect it.

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Who We Are

The Mulligan Report ("we," "us," "our") is a daily golf newsletter published by The Mulligan Report LLC. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you subscribe to our newsletter, visit our website, or interact with us.

What We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to deliver our newsletter and improve your experience:

  • Email address and first name when you subscribe
  • Usage data such as open rates and click activity (anonymized and aggregated)
  • Device and browser information collected automatically through standard web analytics

We do not collect payment information directly. Any transactions are processed through secure third-party providers (Stripe) who maintain their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you The Mulligan Report newsletter
  • To personalize your reading experience
  • To understand how our content performs (aggregate analytics)
  • To communicate with you about your subscription or inquiries
  • To improve our newsletter and website

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Email Communications

By subscribing, you consent to receive our daily newsletter and occasional updates. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link. When you unsubscribe, we remove you from our active mailing list promptly. We use Resend as our email service provider, and your data is processed in accordance with their privacy practices.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality such as remembering your preferences and tracking anonymous site analytics. We do not use invasive tracking or sell data to advertising networks. Third-party embeds (such as YouTube videos) may set their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure email service providers, and limited access to subscriber data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. To make a request, email us at hello@themulliganreport.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are located in the EU, UK, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Contact us and we will accommodate your request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify subscribers via email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@themulliganreport.com.
